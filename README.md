KisanBazaar is a web-based platform built during a hackathon to connect farmers directly with consumers, reducing dependency on middlemen and ensuring fair pricing.

## Problem Statement
Farmers often sell crops through intermediaries due to lack of direct digital access, resulting in low income for farmers and higher prices for consumers.

## Solution
KisanBazaar enables farmers to list crops and consumers to place orders directly. It also provides Minimum Support Price (MSP) comparisons and government scheme information to promote transparency.

## Key Features
- Farmer registration and crop listing
- Consumer marketplace view
- Order placement (demo flow)
- MSP price comparison
- Government schemes information

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap
- Backend: Flask
- Database: SQLite

## Future Scope
- Online payments
- Logistics & delivery integration
- Mobile application
- Farmer verification
